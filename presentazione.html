Statue of Ulisse Dini

The statue of Ulisse Dini is placed in Via Ulisse Dini, close to the Normale. The statue is made up of bronze, the statue rests on a block of marble, in which there is the inscription: "A Ulisse Dini matematico insigne onore dello studio pisano cittadino benemerito Pisa riconoscente 1845-1913".




















The bronze statue was made by sculptor Piedmont Leonardi Bistolfi , and the monument was inaugurated in November 1927.


Who was Ulisse Dini?

Ulisse Dini was an important italian's mathematician; he was born in 1845 on 14 November 1845 in Pisa and he died on 28 October in 1918. Ulisse Dini was an Italian mathematician and politician. He is known for his contribution to real analysis. His studies of real analysis are collected in the book called "Fondamenti per la teorica delle funzioni di variabili reali". Dini attends the Normal School in Pisa, it is student of Henry Betti and it graduates in Mathematics in 1864. Dini attended the Scuola Normale Superiore in order to become a teacher. In 1866, he was appointed to the University of Pisa, where he taught algebra and geodesy. In 1871 his teacher EnricoBetti allows him his desks of Mathematical Analysis and Superior Geometry that he had founded, but Ulisse Dini wanted to research the Infinitesimal Calculation, that experiments in the teaching. In 1871 he enters politics becoming Adviser of the Comune in Pisa. From 1877 they also assign him the desk of Infinitesimal Analysis, he publishes his studies of theory of the functions and in 1880 he is chosen member of the Italian Parliament. In the same year Ulisse publishes a volume on the series of Fourier and from 1888 until 1890, Dini was rettore of the Pisa University, and of the Scuola Normale Superiore from 1908 until his death in 1918. In 1913 founds the School of application for Engineers, the today's Faculty of Engineering. He dies in Pisa 28 October 1918 and he was buried in the Monumental Cemetery. The statue of Ulisse Dini is located in Pisa near his university "Scuola Normale". The bronzy statue was realized by the Piedmontese sculptor Leonardi Bistolfi, and the monument was inaugurated in November 1927.
